A pioneering group of enthusiastic students from IIT Kharagpur, united by a passion for railway engineering. Established under the aegis of the Centre for Railway Research(CRR), TeamTRAK aims to innovate and contribute to the modernization of Indian Railways

Railway Challenge - IMechE

The Railway Challenge provides the real-life experience of the entire cycle of designing and manufacturing a prototype in a structured environment supported by academic and industry mentors. In a fairly risk-free but representative environment, teams and individuals develop key engineering skills alongside business and soft skills to enhance their future in an engineering career. Teams: Develop and demonstrate technical, engineering design and manufacturing skills Obtain practical and rounded railway engineering skills Gain real-world engineering experience before stepping into a job Learn and practice key soft skills such as teamwork and time management alongside project management, budgeting and presentation skills. Becoming better equipped for a long-lasting career in engineering Meet leading professionals in railway engineering and network with potential future employers.

Centre For Railway Research

The Centre for Railway Research was the first such centre set up by the Indian Railways at IIT Kharagpur for a long-term research framework in 2010. Since then, the Centre has taken up multiple projects for the advancement of railway research including High Speed Rail, Heavy Haul Technology, Advanced Materials and Manufacturing, Advanced Maintenance and Operation, among others. The Centre envisions to bridge the gap between Industry and Academia and work on indigenous component development.

About

Who we are

We are the India's first Students' Railway Research group at IIT Kharagpur which embodies a dynamic hub of

  • Innovation
  • Collaboration
  • Creativity

Railway Challenge at IIT Kharagpur took a solid form in December 2023, when a group of enthusiastic students got together to form TeamTRAK. The project is carried out under the aegis of the Centre for Railway Research, IIT Kharagpur. We are guided by our extremely competent faculty, backed by our institute and our sponsors.

Our Mission and Vision

Our main mission is:

  • To contribute to the development of indigenous technologies for Indian Railways
  • To develop skilled workforce in the design and manufacturing of Railway systems
  • To inculcate a spirit of Entrepreneurship in deep-tech areas
  • Represent India and IIT Kharagpur at international forums

As India gears up to usher in a new era of growth in manufacturing industries, there will be a need for talented and skilled manpower who can propel the industry and lead the way. Academic institutions like IIT Kharagpur have a major role to play in shaping budding engineers and entrepreneurs who will play a critical role in these industries. For a country like India, where the needs of a large population need to be catered to with limited resources, efficient Public transport (like Railways) is needed. There is a need for the upgrading of the country’s rail infrastructure, and there is plenty of room for new innovations. In modern days, there is an emphasis on 4Cs- Customer Satisfaction, Capacity, Carbon footprint, and Cost. .

Collaboration

Collaboration is a cornerstone of the group's ethos, as they actively engage with stakeholders across academia, industry, and government agencies to leverage collective expertise and resources.

We as a team, work together to compete on an international level. Our collaborators are

  • Academia: Engaging with academic institutions and researchers.
  • Industry: Partnering with companies and professionals in the railway sector.
  • Government agencies: Working with government bodies for policy support and implementation.

Student Development

The Students' Railway Research Group at IIT Kharagpur offers a unique platform for cultivating the next generation of leaders in the railway sector. By providing ample opportunities for students to lead and manage projects, the group ensures that they are well-prepared for future leadership roles.The skills are developed through-

  • Comprehensive Training: Students receive thorough training in research methodologies and techniques, enabling them to conduct detailed and accurate investigations.
  • Access to Resources: The group provides access to extensive research materials, databases, and state-of-the-art laboratory facilities.
  • Mentorship: Faculty mentors guide students through the research process, offering expertise and feedback to refine their research skills.
